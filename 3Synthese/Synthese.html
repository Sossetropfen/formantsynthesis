What happened here?

The formant synthesis is based on the source-filter-model of speech generation. Here, voiced and unvoiced source signals are modified through so-called formants. This approach aims to duplicate all of the parameters, that are used in human speech production, through using formant filters. The value of each individual formant filter changes in correlation to the different phonemes and sounds.

(cf. O‘Shaughnessy, 2000)

Types of speech synthesis

There are other variants of speech signal generators that each come with their own benefits and disadvantages. The unit-selection synthesis and the PSOLA synthesis represent concatenive synthesis’, while the “Hidden Markov Model”-based synthesis depicts even another approach to generate speech signals.
